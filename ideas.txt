PROBLEM:
-What information can be gained from a set of worn stairs.
-How often were the stairs used?
-Was a direction of travel preferred?
-How many people used the stairs simultaneously?

EXTRA:
-Assume we have estimates for age; usage; day-to-day life of those in the structure.
-Is the wear consistent with the data provided?
-Is the age of the stairwell consistent with its use?
-Has it been repaired?
-Can you determine what material has been used?
-Can you determine the number of people using the stairs day-to-day?


INPUTS:
-Approximate age
-Material
-Measurements of height, depth, etc. at different points.
-Location
-Inside/Outside


OUTPUTS
-How frequently were the stairs used
-Which direction preferred
-How many people used the stairs simultaneously?
